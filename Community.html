Community

Active over 75s is not just about sports, we also act as a social club for our members. We believe a healthy lifestyle is all about balance. We know that life isn't all about exercise, you need a bit of time to relax and make new friends. For this reason we provide our members with social events too like our morning coffee and bingo nights!

Coffee Mornings

The coffee mornings act as way for members to interact with another socially, at these events tea and coffee are provided along with biscuits. For Gold members there is a coffee morning from 07:00 to 10:00 every Tuesday. For Silver members the coffee morning is held the first saturday of the month from 07:00 to 10:00. For bronze members the coffee morning dates will be posted o the noticeboard

Bingo Nights

These will be held in the Club Cafe every second Tuesday at 20:00. There will be a €3 entry fee for members and you are welcome to bring a guest along with you (non members: €5). There will be cash prizes along with a glass of wine or tea provided. All memebers are welcome to attend

Book Club

The book club was started to provide a our members with the oppurtunity to socialize outside of sports. To sign up just go to reception and enquire.

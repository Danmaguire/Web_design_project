Other Events

Tournaments

There will be bi-monthly tennis and golf tournaments, these are completely optional and allow for the creation of a fun competitive enviroment for those who choose to compete. Tournamet detals will be posted on the noticeboard two weeks before the tournament is due to start and sign ups will be taken at the desk up till the week before the contest

Coffee Mornings

These will be held in the club cafe. There will be coffee provided, other light refreshments will also be available

Bingo Nights

On every second tuesday a bingo night will be hosted in the club cafe at 20:00 , all memebers are welcome to attend

Book Club

Will be held in the club cafe, meetings will be held every wedensday and friday

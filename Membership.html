Membership Rates (2016)

Below are outlines of what membership types include.

  • Gold Membership
    • Entitles the member to unlimited group activites per month
    • 2 private coaching sessions per month
    • Gold member coffee morning every Tuesday
    • Access to VIP changing facilities
    • Price: €200 per year

  • Silver Membership
    • Entitles the member to up to 5 group activites per month
    • 1 private coaching session per month
    • Monthly coffee mornings
    • Access to changing facilities
    • Complimentary towels
    • Price: €175 per year

  • Bronze Membership
    • Entitles the member to up to 3 group activites per month
    • 1 private coaching session every 3 months
    • Bi-yearly coffee mornings
    • Access to changing facilities
    • Price: €150 per year

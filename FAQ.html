Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is Active Over 75's (AO75s)?

    2. AO75s is a local sports club catering to those over 75. Sports range from hill walking to swimming and few others inbetween.

  2. Who can join?

    3. All those over the age of 75, who are in good health are more than welcome to joing AO75s

  3. How can i join?

    4. You can either come to the front desk or you can go to our membership page to fill our the membership form.

  4. How much is membership?

    5. The membership prices range from €200 - €150 depending on your choice of membership. More details are avilable in the Membership Rates section of our membership page.

  5. Im not very active will that be a problem?

    6. No not at all, the activities range in terms of how physically demandig they are. There is also never any pressure at AO75s to push yourself, we are all about have an enjoyable time whie getting fit.

  6. What facilitties are available?

    7. At the AO75s facility we have a 20m swimming pool, 2 yoga studios, 4 clay tennis courts and 2 grass tennis courts. We also boast an award winning 9 hole golf course ad a cafe.

  7. How do i get in contact?

    8. Our contact details are available on our Contact page.

  8. Is car parking available?

    9. We have a carpark that can cater for 150 cars and we also have 15 disabled bays next to the enterance of our facility.

  9. Where is AO75s based?

    10. AO75s is located in the heart of Dublin city, more details can be found on our Contact Page

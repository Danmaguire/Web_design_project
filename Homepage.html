Welcome to Active Over 75s

Active Over 75s (AO75s) is a multi sports club catering for the over 75s, with sports ranging from hill walking to tennis and a few more inbetween. The goal of AO75s is to encourae a healthy lifesytle for participants, and also to create a social enviroment for our members. We also host many social events such as bingo nights, a book club and also tournaments

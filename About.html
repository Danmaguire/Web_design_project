About Actice Over 75s

Learn a little about what we are all about.

At active over 75's we believe that you can and should stay active at any age. We provide a range of activities from yoga to hiking. Our classes our open to everyone no matter what your activity level is! We create a fun and engaging environment for our over 75 actives. Our classes are top notch, our instructors are patient and kind, and our facility is only the best. Here at Active Over 75's we believe that you deserve the best and we are alway here to help.

Our Aims:

  • To improve quality of life through exercise and fun
  • To create a sense of community
  • To give encouragement and support
  • To allow our members to set goals and achieve them
